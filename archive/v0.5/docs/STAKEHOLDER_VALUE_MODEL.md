# Stakeholder Value Model

The demo uses transparent assumptions to show why Bankable Property Network matters for banks, buyers, developers, compliance, and regulators.

## Bank

Assumptions:

```text
annual foreign buyer flow influenced: 1,000 deals
average deal size: 12M THB
bank-captured flow at 10% adoption: 1.2B THB
escrow/compliance fee at 0.25%: 3M THB revenue
manual review time saved: 2 hours per case
cases requiring escalation reduced: 30%
```

Benefits:

- captures high-value property settlement flow before it leaves bankable rails;
- creates a premium escrow and compliance product;
- reduces manual review time with structured evidence packs;
- converts foreign buyers into bankable clients;
- creates audit visibility before funds move.

## Buyer

- avoids sending irreversible deposits to unclear legal entities;
- sees capital sources classified as green, amber, or red;
- receives an evidence pack for bank, lawyer, developer, or dispute follow-up;
- becomes less dependent on an unverified agent.

## Developer

- receives a stronger buyer signal;
- gets cleaner settlement instructions;
- reduces friction around source-of-funds review;
- can accept escrow release conditions instead of ad hoc payment pressure.

## Compliance And Regulator

- gets visibility before the money moves;
- can inspect rule-based decisions and human approvals;
- can verify audit trail integrity without seeing sensitive data on-chain.
