# Bank Journey

## Goal

Help a banking anchor capture high-value settlement flow while managing risk, documentation, and customer trust.

## Flow

1. Bank receives buyer/property intake.
2. System maps originator bank, receiving bank, buyer capital, and purpose of transfer.
3. Bank officer reviews Capital Bankability Map.
4. System proposes FET-ready route, escrow conditions, or rejection/escalation.
5. Bank officer sends counter-offer to buyer.
6. Compliance reviews evidence and approves, rejects, or escalates.
7. Bank retains Closing Passport evidence hash and summary.

## Bank Outcomes

- Captured flow from bankable settlement routes.
- Reduced incomplete document packets.
- Better buyer onboarding.
- Audit-ready evidence.
- Post-purchase financial service opportunity.

## Bank Boundary

The bank validates settlement readiness and controls release conditions. It does not guarantee legal title or replace lawyers, regulators, or land offices.
