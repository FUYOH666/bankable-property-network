# Compliance Journey

## Goal

Give compliance teams a structured review path for property settlement risk before money moves.

## Flow

1. Compliance receives a scenario with buyer, property, agent, developer, capital, and payment instruction data.
2. System presents risk flags and source documents.
3. Compliance reviews capital status: green, amber, red, or mixed.
4. Compliance reviews property and agent risk.
5. Compliance selects approve, conditional approval, reject, or escalate.
6. Closing Passport captures evidence hash and approval role.

## Compliance Outputs

- Approval memo.
- Escrow conditions.
- Evidence pack.
- Escalation reason.
- Attestation metadata.

## Compliance Boundary

Model output is advisory. Final decision remains human-in-the-loop and role-based.
