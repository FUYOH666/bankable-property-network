## FET

**Q: What is FET (Foreign Exchange Transaction)?**  
A: A Thai bank document confirming inbound foreign currency for property purchase. Required for foreign freehold quota purchases. Landmark tier-1 path supports **FET-ready escrow** on the demo stack.

**Q: Can the WhatsApp consultant approve my deposit?**  
A: **No.** Consult explains project facts from the developer KB. Only **bankable escrow** on a **verified authorized payee** (Bangkok Landmark Group Public Co., Ltd.) may be considered by the bank — consult never instructs you to wire to an agent account.
