# Sales Process — Landmark Sukhumvit Tower

## Step 1 — Inquiry (consult layer)

Buyer explores **Landmark Sukhumvit Tower** via WhatsApp, web, or API. Consult cites project KB: location, unit types, price bands, FET basics, installment summary.

## Step 2 — Reservation (developer channel)

- Reservation with **Bangkok Landmark Group Public Co., Ltd.** (licensed sales entity).
- Booking deposit per demo terms: **10%** of unit price.
- Payment instructions must show **Bangkok Landmark Group Public Co., Ltd.** as payee — match Developer Knowledge Hub feed.

## Step 3 — Contract and installments

- **15%** on SPA signing; staged progress payments; balance on transfer.
- Foreign buyers: plan for **FET-ready** inbound transfer before freehold registration.

## Step 4 — Bankable handoff

When significant funds move, Bankable Property OS engages:

1. Capital classification (SWIFT / USDT / cash-P2P).
2. Payee verification vs developer ERP feed.
3. Route decision: approve / conditional / reject / escalate.
4. Escrow and Closing Passport on permitted routes only.

## Consult guardrail

Consult **never** confirms a payee or approves a deposit. Off-platform prelaunch projects (e.g. Shadow Bay in supplier contrast) are **not** this inventory — they illustrate why verified feeds matter.
