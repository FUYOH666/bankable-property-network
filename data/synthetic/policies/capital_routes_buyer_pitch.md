## USDT (amber) — key message

USDT is **not** a shortcut around the bank. It is amber capital: conversion evidence, verified payee, bank review, then FET-ready escrow — same Closing Passport discipline as SWIFT.

## Cash / P2P (red) — key message

Cash-like P2P routes lack bankable trail. Demo outcome: reject or escalate; no deposit guidance; Closing Passport not generated.

## Mixed capital — key message

Each source is classified separately. Partial approval may apply; never wire to agent personal accounts.
