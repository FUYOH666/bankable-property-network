# Prelaunch Sales Policy

Data classification: synthetic demo data.

## Principle

No bankable settlement for off-plan or prelaunch unit sales until:

1. Construction permit status is verified.
2. EIA clearance is documented where applicable.
3. Licensed sales entity matches authorized payee in Developer Knowledge Hub.
4. Buyer protection and refund terms are in evidence pack.

## Off-platform sales

Agents marketing units without hub connection must not receive bank-routed deposits. Property Shield escalates prelaunch_without_permit signals.

## On-network tier-1 developers

Developers with verified ERP feeds and issued permits may receive standard escrow routing subject to capital classification.
