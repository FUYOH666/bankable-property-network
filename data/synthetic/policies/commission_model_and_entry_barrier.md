# Commission Model And Professional Entry Barrier

Data classification: synthetic demo data.

These snippets model the structural market failure that Bankable Property OS addresses.

## Developer-Paid Commissions

Thailand property sales often rely on developer-paid commissions. Any intermediary who brings a buyer may participate in a transaction. The incentive structure favors deal volume over settlement quality.

Operational risk:

- payment instructions may come from unverified intermediaries;
- payee legal authority may be unclear;
- banks and regulators lose visibility before funds move.

## No Professional Entry Barrier

Intermediaries are typically not verified for:

- local property law and ownership limits;
- visa and foreign buyer rules;
- FET and foreign currency inflow requirements;
- building bylaws and rental restrictions;
- payee authority and escrow readiness.

A person may enter the market without demonstrated competence yet still receive commission-driven payment instructions.

## Money Off Bankable Rails

When settlement happens outside bank-controlled routes:

- source-of-funds review is fragmented;
- grey P2P or cash-like routes increase;
- disputed deposits become harder to trace;
- affluent foreign capital may bypass banking anchors.

## Kingdom Brand Gap

Foreign capital expects institutional-grade settlement aligned with Thailand's Kingdom brand promise: solid, quality, technologically capable markets.

Market reality may include commission-driven intermediaries without a verification layer, reputational drag from disputed deposits, and inconsistent foreign inflow evidence.

## Infrastructure Response

Bankable Property Network introduces:

- verified participant signals for developers and agents;
- capital bankability classification;
- settlement route comparison with bankable escrow preference;
- human compliance approval before release;
- Closing Passport evidence hash without sensitive data on-chain;
- AI-assisted evidence retrieval in a controlled environment.

Primary customer: banking anchor and regulated money-serving structures. Buyer protection is a social bonus when infrastructure works correctly.
