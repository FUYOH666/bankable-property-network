## Concepts (informational)

### FET (Foreign Exchange Transaction)

Thai banks record inbound foreign currency for property purchases by non-residents. Banks use FET documentation as part of freehold eligibility review. Demo uses FET-ready escrow as a green/amber outcome — not a guarantee of ownership.

### Land Department transfer registration

Property ownership or leasehold rights are registered with the Department of Lands after compliant payment and developer/seller documentation. Bankable settlement aims to align payee, escrow, and registration evidence.

### Condominium Act — foreign quota

Foreign freehold in condominiums is subject to quota and eligibility rules under Thai law. Demo references the concept only; it does not assess buyer eligibility.
