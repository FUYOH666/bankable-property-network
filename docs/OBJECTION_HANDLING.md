# Objection Handling

## Are You Replacing Lawyers?

No. Bankable Property Network is an evidence and settlement readiness layer. It helps identify risks before money moves, but it does not provide legal advice or replace lawyers, land offices, or title checks.

## Are You Doing KYC?

No. The demo classifies synthetic capital sources. In production, KYC/KYB remains with regulated providers and banks. The platform can integrate with those providers, but final responsibility remains with the appropriate regulated party.

## Why Blockchain?

We do not tokenize the property. We tokenize the evidence of a verified settlement process.

The attestation contains metadata and an evidence hash. It does not contain passports, contracts, raw bank statements, or personal data.

## Why Not Just Use A Listing Marketplace?

Listing marketplaces help buyers discover property. Discovery is not trust.

Bankable Property Network focuses on the transaction layer: verified participants, capital classification, settlement route, escrow conditions, compliance review, and evidence.

## Why Would A Bank Want This?

The bank can capture high-value settlement flow before it leaves bankable rails, onboard foreign buyers, sell escrow/compliance packages, reduce reputational risk, and create post-purchase financial relationships.

## What Happens With Cash Or P2P?

The platform should not route unsupported cash-like flows. The demo marks those routes red and returns reject or escalation.

## What Happens With USDT?

USDT is amber by default. It can become bankable only after wallet review, source explanation, and conversion into an approved settlement path.

## What Is Production Risk?

The largest risk is overclaiming. The platform must avoid claiming legal title approval, regulator replacement, KYC replacement, or fraud guarantee.
