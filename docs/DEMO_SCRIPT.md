# Demo Script

## One-Liner

Bankable Property Network is bank-grade money infrastructure for Thailand property — verification, settlement routing, escrow controls, and evidence for banking anchors before funds move.

## 3-Minute Flow

See also: `docs/HACKATHON_RUNBOOK.md`, `docs/MONEY_INFRASTRUCTURE_THESIS.md`, `docs/AI_AUDIT_INDEX.md`.

### 0:00-0:40 — Brand And Structural Gap

Thailand's property market runs on developer-paid commissions with no professional entry barrier. Intermediaries are not verified for FET, visa rules, payee authority, or local law. Money flows off bankable rails while the Kingdom brand promise expects institutional-grade settlement.

Developers hold inventory and payee truth; agents often do not. Off-platform prelaunch can hide permit and payee gaps.

### 0:40-1:20 — Money OS And AI Layer

Bankable Property OS gives banks and regulated structures an operating layer: verify participants, classify capital, route settlement, control escrow, scale evidence review with AI, and gate release through human compliance.

AI does not decide whether money moves. AI helps regulated structures review evidence faster, at scale, with traceability.

### 1:20-2:20 — Live Demo

**Supplier Contrast** — Shadow Bay prelaunch (off-platform, no permit) vs Bangkok Landmark Group (fictional tier-1 on-network). See `docs/DEVELOPER_SUPPLY_DEMO.md`.

Anchor case illustrates **infrastructure failure** — not buyer blame: 12M THB Bangkok condo, unverified settlement path, payee mismatch, deposit pressure.

The system shows:

0. Supplier Contrast — prelaunch without permit vs tier-1 verified developer feed.
1. Developer Knowledge Hub — agent payee instruction vs developer authorized feed (mismatch detected).
2. Property Shield flags payment mismatch and urgency.
3. Capital Bankability Map classifies SWIFT, USDT, and cash/P2P differently.
4. Route Comparison rejects direct deposit and recommends bankable escrow.
5. Bank Counter-Offer keeps settlement on compliant rails.
6. Compliance sees evidence and RAG trace (Scenario Simulator — eight synthetic paths).
7. Closing Passport is generated only for a permitted route.
8. Post-Closing Yield Plan previews long-term asset operations on bank rails.

### 2:20-2:50 — Multi-Stakeholder Value

- Bank: capture flow, escrow/compliance, long-term relationship.
- Compliance: structured gate, audit trail.
- State/brand: traceable inflows, market quality alignment.
- Developers on-network: SSOT feed before buyers arrive.
- Verified participants: differentiation from commission-only intermediaries.
- Buyer: social bonus — deposit protection on bankable rails.

### 2:50-3:00 — Closing Passport And Close

The system generates a Closing Passport: an evidence pack hash and metadata-only attestation.

We do not tokenize the property. We tokenize the evidence of a verified settlement process.

## Closing Line

Everyone is building faster ways to move money. We are building the layer that decides whether money should move, under what verified conditions, and with what evidence.

Most platforms stop when the property is sold. Banks should not.

Settlement OS without truth upstream is incomplete. Developer SSOT → verified agents → bank rails → Closing Passport → Yield OS.
